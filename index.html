🌱 Welcome to Grass Roots Cyber!
Hey there, digital wanderer — you've just stumbled upon the buzzing hive of cyber goodness built from the ground up — literally. Welcome to Grass Roots Cyber, where digital resilience meets down-to-earth community power.

We’re not your typical cybersecurity crew. We're a nonprofit collective of tinkerers, teachers, tech nerds, and first responders—on a mission to make cybersecurity accessible, understandable, and actionable for everyone. From small-town municipalities and fire departments to K-12 schools and local nonprofits — we believe every community deserves the tools to thrive safely in the digital world.

🛠️ What we do:
Workshops, simulations, hands-on training, and even tournaments (yeah, we gamify it) — all designed to make cyber literacy fun, useful, and real-world ready. Whether you’re patching up your password habits or prepping your team for incident response, we’ve got your back.

⚡ Why it matters:
Hackers don’t discriminate — but access to cybersecurity education often does. That’s why we serve where others overlook. No jargon. No gatekeeping. Just practical defense for the digital neighborhood.

🎯 Here’s the big picture:
A future where every small town, school, and org — regardless of budget — has the knowledge, confidence, and tech-savvy community to stand strong against digital threats.

So whether you’re curious, cautious, or just totally confused about cybersecurity — you’re in the right place. Browse around, reach out, or partner up. We’re building something better. Together. From the roots up.

🌾 Let’s grow cyber strong, community by community.
